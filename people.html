Rui (Ray) Yang, MD, PhD
Dr. Rui (Ray) Yang is a board-certified pediatrician and incoming tenure-track assistant professor at Baylor College of Medicine (BCM). He will launch his laboratory at Texas Children’s Hospital in summer 2026. His research focuses on monogenic causes of severe infection, immune dysregulation, and malignancy.
